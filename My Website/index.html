Personal Interests

I love music. I have always loved listening to music and escaping in a song. My favorite bands are always changing. I've been playing guitar off and on since I was 13. I may sound like I just picked up a guitar a few months ago, but I love it none the less.

I have been studying computer science at UVU since 2018. I feel like I've made very little progress since the pandemic hit in 2020. I am very much looking forward to being back at campus this Fall. I feel like I am more committed when I am there. I am too much of a slacker when it comes to live streaming classes. I feel like programming is very rewarding when I can get something to work. However, I find that I struggle a lot with programming and I often wonder if I am cut out for it.

I enjoy spending time with my lovely fiancé, Alexis, who I will be marrying in June. Together we have an adorable border collie named Chewbert or Chewy for short. We like to cook delicious meals together and hangout with our friends. We also enjoy playing badminton and taking Chewy on walks.
